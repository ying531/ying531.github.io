Hi! I am a final-year PhD student at Department of Statistics, Stanford University. I am fortunate to be advised by Professors Emmanuel Candès and Dominik Rothenhäusler. Here are my CV, Github and Google scholar pages.

My research focuses on statistical methodology for trusted inference and decisions. I design methods that provide solid guarantees for the use of black-box prediction models in real decisions such as drug discovery. Additionally, I work on addressing distribution shifts – of various forms – that arise in predictive inference, causal inference, replicability, and offline policy learning, with a recent focus on their empirical grounds.

I currently help organize the Online Causal Inference Seminar.

I’m on the 2023-2024 job market!
