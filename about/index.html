About Me

 

I am a second-year PhD student at Stanford Statistics. I am fortunate to be advised by Professors Emmanuel Candès and Dominik Rothenhäusler. I was also mentored by Professor Stefan Wager on particular research projects.

Prior to Stanford, I earned my BS in Mathematics and a double degree in Ecomonics (Finance), both from Tsinghua University, China. I was advised by Professor Dong Li on my undergraduate research and thesis. I am also fortunate to be advised by Professors Zhaoran Wang and Junwei Lu since my undergraduate years on projects about graphical models and more recently reinforcement learning and causality.

Beyond academics, I love traveling and photography in my free time. See my photography gallery.
